Hint:

You can create your own formats. Check out the following special symbols:

dt = Date-time of the message.
u = Sender's name.
m = Message body.
\n = New line.
\t = Tab character.


For date formatting you can use the following special characters:

Y = Year.
M = Month as a number (01 for January, etc).
D = Day.
H = Hours in 24-hours format.
h = Hours in 12-hours format.
m = Minutes.
s = Seconds.
a = "a.m." or "p.m."
